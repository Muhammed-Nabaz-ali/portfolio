Cee Speed Camera & GPS

Cee Speed Camera & GPS is a globally accessible map application designed to enhance driver awareness and road safety by pinpointing the locations of speed cameras. The app provides real-time updates and displays speed camera locations with intuitive icons on the map, specifically well-moderated in Kurdistan for accuracy. Equipped with geofencing technology, Cee sends timely alerts as drivers approach camera zones, ensuring they stay informed and compliant. This feature-rich app combines user-friendly design with essential road safety tools, making it an indispensable companion for mindful driving.
